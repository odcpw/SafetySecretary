## de
Title: Agent-Grenzen
Audience: Nutzer, die wissen muessen, was die KI darf
Summary: Die KI hilft beim Schreiben und Fragen, entscheidet aber nicht.
How to:
- Behandle KI-Ausgaben als Entwurf.
- Bestaetige Risiken, Bewertungen und Kontrollen selbst.
- Nutze keine KI-Frage als Freigabeersatz.
See also: privacy, hira, incident-investigation, faq
Body:
Safety Secretary speichert Nutzerentscheidungen, nicht KI-Urteile. Die spaetere Fragefunktion fuer Hilfeartikel ist nicht Teil dieser v1-Hilfe.

## en
Title: Agent boundaries
Audience: Users who need to know what the assistant may do
Summary: The assistant helps draft, ask, and summarize; it does not decide.
How to:
- Treat assistant output as a draft.
- Confirm hazards, ratings, and controls yourself.
- Never treat an assistant answer as approval.
See also: privacy, hira, incident-investigation, faq
Body:
Safety Secretary records user decisions, not AI authority. The later ability to ask about a help section is outside this v1 static help surface.

## fr
Title: Limites assistant
Audience: Utilisateurs qui veulent comprendre le role IA
Summary: L'assistant aide a rediger et resumer; il ne decide pas.
How to:
- Traite les sorties IA comme brouillon.
- Confirme toi-meme dangers, evaluations et controles.
- Ne remplace pas une approbation par une reponse IA.
See also: privacy, hira, incident-investigation, faq
Body:
Safety Secretary enregistre les decisions des utilisateurs, pas une autorite IA. La future question sur une section d'aide n'est pas incluse ici.

## it
Title: Limiti assistente
Audience: Utenti che vogliono capire il ruolo IA
Summary: L'assistente aiuta a scrivere e riassumere; non decide.
How to:
- Tratta l'output IA come bozza.
- Conferma tu pericoli, valutazioni e controlli.
- Non usare una risposta IA come approvazione.
See also: privacy, hira, incident-investigation, faq
Body:
Safety Secretary registra decisioni degli utenti, non autorita IA. La futura domanda su una sezione di aiuto non fa parte di questa guida statica v1.
