## de
Title: Incident Investigation
Audience: Teams nach Ereignissen oder Beinaheereignissen
Summary: II erfasst Zeitleiste, Zeugen, 5-Whys, Fotos und Massnahmen.
How to:
- Baue die Zeitleiste aus Ereignissen, Aussagen und Belegen.
- Erfasse den tatsaechlichen Ausgang getrennt von der glaubwuerdig schlimmsten realistischen Auswirkung.
- Haenge Fotos an relevante Timeline-Eintraege.
- Nutze 5-Whys als Coaching und pruefe HIRA-Follow-up.
See also: actions, hira, privacy, exports
Body:
II startet ohne HIRA-Suche und kann spaeter mit einer HIRA verknuepft werden. Die Untersuchungsprioritaet folgt SIF/pSIF-Denken: Ein Ereignis mit kleiner tatsaechlicher Verletzung kann trotzdem hohes Potenzial haben. Kommunikationsausgaben koennen ausgewaehlte Timeline-Fotos einbetten.

## en
Title: Incident investigation
Audience: Teams after incidents or near misses
Summary: II captures timeline, witnesses, 5-Whys, photos, evidence, and corrective actions.
How to:
- Build the timeline from events, statements, and evidence.
- Record actual harm separately from the credible worst realistic potential outcome.
- Attach incident photos to relevant timeline entries.
- Use 5-Whys as coaching and review HIRA follow-up.
See also: actions, hira, privacy, exports
Body:
II starts with a HIRA follow-up placeholder and later connects to real HIRA search. Investigation priority follows SIF/pSIF thinking: a low-harm event can still have high potential. Communication outputs may embed selected timeline photos.

## fr
Title: Investigation incident
Audience: Equipes apres incident ou presque accident
Summary: II capture chronologie, temoins, 5-Whys, photos, preuves et actions.
How to:
- Construis la chronologie avec evenements, temoignages et preuves.
- Separe le dommage reel de l'issue credible la plus grave.
- Attache les photos aux entrees de timeline pertinentes.
- Utilise 5-Whys comme coaching et verifie le suivi HIRA.
See also: actions, hira, privacy, exports
Body:
II commence avec un suivi HIRA en texte libre et se relie plus tard a une vraie recherche HIRA. La priorite d'enquete suit la logique SIF/pSIF: un evenement avec peu de dommage reel peut avoir un potentiel eleve. Les communications peuvent integrer des photos selectionnees.

## it
Title: Indagine incidente
Audience: Team dopo incidenti o quasi incidenti
Summary: II cattura timeline, testimoni, 5-Whys, foto, prove e azioni correttive.
How to:
- Costruisci la timeline con eventi, dichiarazioni e prove.
- Registra il danno effettivo separato dall'esito credibile peggiore realistico.
- Collega foto alle voci timeline rilevanti.
- Usa 5-Whys come coaching e rivedi follow-up HIRA.
See also: actions, hira, privacy, exports
Body:
II parte con un placeholder follow-up HIRA e poi si collega alla ricerca HIRA reale. La priorita dell'indagine segue il pensiero SIF/pSIF: un evento con poco danno effettivo puo comunque avere alto potenziale. Le comunicazioni possono includere foto timeline selezionate.
